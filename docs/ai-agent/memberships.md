# Memberships

## What Is a Membership?

A membership is offered to gymnasts who train regularly, typically those involved in competitive gymnastics. It guarantees your gymnast a place in their sessions each week and includes a small discount compared to paying per session.

Memberships are calculated on a 46-week year to account for seasonal closures, occasional illness, and other holidays.

**Example questions:**
- *What is a membership?* — A membership guarantees your gymnast a weekly slot and offers a small discount on the session rate. It's available to gymnasts who train regularly.
- *How much does a membership cost?* — The price depends on how many sessions your gymnast attends each week. Message us on WhatsApp at https://wa.me/447700149040 for a quote.
- *Is a membership better value?* — If your gymnast trains consistently every week, a membership offers a small saving over pay-as-you-go. It also guarantees their place in the session.

## How Memberships Work

Memberships are paid monthly in advance. The monthly amount is calculated based on the session rate across 46 weeks per year, divided into 12 equal payments.

Because the cost is spread evenly across the year (including closure periods), individual sessions within a membership cannot be cancelled for a credit — the discount already accounts for weeks where training doesn't take place.

**Example questions:**
- *Can I cancel individual sessions on a membership?* — No. Because the membership price is calculated across the full year including closures and breaks, individual sessions can't be cancelled for a credit.
- *How is the monthly price calculated?* — It's based on the session rate across 46 weeks of the year, spread across 12 monthly payments.

## Cancelling a Membership

You can cancel a membership at any time. It will not renew after cancellation and remains active until the end of the current paid period.

**Example questions:**
- *Can I cancel my membership?* — Yes, at any time. It stays active until the end of the period you've paid for and then stops.
- *What happens if I cancel mid-month?* — The membership stays active until the end of that paid period. No refund is given for the remaining time.

## Standing Slots

A membership comes with standing slots — your gymnast's regular place in a specific session each week. Standing slots are managed by the club. If your gymnast won't be attending their regular session on a particular week, let the club know in advance so the place can be offered to someone else.

**Example questions:**
- *What is a standing slot?* — It's your gymnast's regular, reserved place in a weekly session, included with their membership.
