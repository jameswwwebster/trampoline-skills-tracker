# Competitions

## Overview

Trampoline Life enters gymnasts into external competitions throughout the year. Participation is by invitation only — coaches assess each gymnast's readiness before sending an invite. Not every gymnast will be invited to every competition, and that's normal.

## How Invitations Work

When a gymnast is selected for a competition, their guardian receives an email notification and a message in the app. The invitation includes the entry fee set by the coach.

To respond, go to **My Competitions** in the Bookings menu and choose **Accept** or **Decline**.

- **Accept** — confirms you want to enter. The entry charge is added to **My Charges** straight away and can be paid through the cart.
- **Decline** — removes the gymnast from the entry list. No charge is created.

Entries are not submitted to the competition organiser until payment is complete.

**Example questions:**
- *My child has been invited to a competition, what do I do?* — Go to My Competitions in the Bookings menu and tap Accept or Decline. If you accept, the entry fee will appear in My Charges and can be paid through the cart.
- *How do I pay for a competition entry?* — Once you accept the invitation, the charge appears under My Charges in the Bookings menu. Add it to your cart and check out to pay.
- *How does my coach decide who gets invited?* — Coaches assess each gymnast's readiness individually. Different competitions have different level requirements, from beginner-friendly events to higher-performance competitions.

## Synchro Entries

If a gymnast is competing as part of a synchronised pair, both gymnasts receive separate invitations. Each guardian accepts independently and pays their own entry fee.

## Refunds

Competition entry fees are generally non-refundable once submitted to the competition organiser. If you need to withdraw after paying, contact us on WhatsApp at https://wa.me/447700149040 and we will do what we can, but refunds depend on the organiser's policy and whether the entry has been submitted.

**Example questions:**
- *Can I get a refund if my child can no longer attend a competition?* — Competition fees are generally non-refundable once submitted to the organiser. Contact us on WhatsApp at https://wa.me/447700149040 and we'll check what's possible.
- *What if I decline after accepting?* — If payment hasn't been made yet, simply don't pay and let us know. If the entry has already been submitted and paid, it may not be refundable.

## Unpaid Competition Charges

Outstanding competition charges appear under **My Charges** in the Bookings menu. Unpaid charges will eventually block further bookings, so it's important to settle them promptly.

**Example questions:**
- *I accepted a competition invite but haven't paid yet, will anything happen?* — Yes — outstanding charges will block new bookings if left unpaid. Go to My Charges and pay through the cart.
- *Where do I find outstanding charges?* — Under **My Charges** in the Bookings menu. Add them to your cart and check out to pay.
