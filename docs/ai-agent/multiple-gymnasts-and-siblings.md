# Multiple Gymnasts & Siblings

## Adding Multiple Gymnasts

You can add more than one gymnast to your account and manage all their bookings in one place. When booking, you can select which gymnasts to include and check out for all of them in a single transaction.

**Example questions:**
- *Can I manage multiple children on one account?* — Yes. Add each gymnast from the Account page and book sessions for all of them together.
- *How do I add another gymnast to my account?* — Go to the Account page in the app and use the option to add a gymnast.
- *Can I book sessions for two children at the same time?* — Yes. At the session page, select both gymnasts and they'll be added to your cart together.

## British Gymnastics Numbers

Each gymnast needs their own British Gymnastics membership number. Numbers are entered individually on the Account page against each gymnast.

**Example questions:**
- *Do both my children need a BG number?* — Yes, each gymnast needs their own British Gymnastics membership number. Enter each one on the Account page.

## Sibling Discount

There is no sibling discount. All gymnasts are charged at the standard session rate.

**Example questions:**
- *Is there a discount for siblings?* — No, all gymnasts are charged at the standard rate of £6 per session.
