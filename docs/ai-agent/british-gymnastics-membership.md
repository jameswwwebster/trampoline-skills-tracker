# British Gymnastics Membership

## Why It's Required

All gymnasts must hold a valid British Gymnastics (BG) membership to continue training. BG membership provides insurance cover for your child during sessions and is a requirement of our club affiliation.

**Example questions:**
- *Why does my child need a British Gymnastics number?* — BG membership provides insurance cover for all gymnasts training at our club. It's a requirement of our affiliation with British Gymnastics.
- *Does my child need BG membership to start?* — Your child can attend their first two sessions without one, but a BG number must be added before they can book further sessions.

## The Two-Session Grace

New gymnasts can book up to two sessions before a BG number is required. After that, bookings will be blocked until a valid number is added to their profile.

**Example questions:**
- *My child is new — do they need BG membership straight away?* — No, they can attend their first two sessions without one. After that, a BG number is required.
- *How many sessions can my child do without a BG number?* — Two sessions. After that, further bookings require a valid BG number.

## How to Add a BG Number

Go to the **Account** page in the app, find the gymnast, and enter their BG membership number. Once submitted, the number is checked against British Gymnastics records.

While verification is in progress, you have a **14-day grace period** during which bookings can continue as normal.

You can get a BG membership at https://www.british-gymnastics.org

**Example questions:**
- *How do I add a BG number?* — Go to the Account page, find the gymnast, and enter the number in the BG membership section.
- *Where do I get a British Gymnastics membership?* — Visit https://www.british-gymnastics.org to register.
- *Can my child still train while the number is being verified?* — Yes. You have a 14-day grace period from when the number was entered while verification is in progress.

## Membership Statuses

- **Verified** — the number has been confirmed. No action needed.
- **Pending** — the number has been entered and is being checked. Bookings can continue during the grace period.
- **Invalid** — the number could not be matched. Bookings are blocked until a correct number is entered.

If a previously invalid number is corrected, the grace period is shortened to 3 days.

**Example questions:**
- *My child's BG status says Pending — is that OK?* — Yes, it means we're still verifying the number. Your child can continue booking during the 14-day grace period.
- *My child's BG status says Invalid — what do I do?* — Check the number on the Account page and re-enter it. Bookings are blocked until a valid number is confirmed.
- *My child's status has been Pending for a long time — what's wrong?* — Double-check the number was entered correctly on the Account page. If it looks right, message us on WhatsApp at https://wa.me/447700149040 and we'll look into it.
