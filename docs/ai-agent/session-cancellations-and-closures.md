# Session Cancellations & Closures (Club Side)

## Club Closures

The club closes periodically for school holidays, bank holidays, and other planned breaks. Closures are announced on the **Noticeboard** and are reflected in the booking calendar — sessions cannot be booked during a closure period.

**Example questions:**
- *Is the club open during school holidays?* — We close for some holidays and breaks. Check the Noticeboard or the booking calendar for closure dates.
- *How do I find out about upcoming closures?* — Closures are posted on the Noticeboard in the app and shown on the booking calendar. You won't be able to book sessions during a closure.
- *Will I be notified of closures?* — Closure announcements appear on the Noticeboard. Keep an eye on the app for updates.

## What to Bring

- Comfortable sportswear
- Sports shoes (no bare feet)
- Socks are required at all times on the trampoline
- Long hair must be tied back
- A drink

**Example questions:**
- *What should my child wear?* — Comfortable sportswear and sports shoes. Socks are required on the trampoline.
- *Do I need to bring anything?* — Sports shoes, socks, and a drink. Long hair should be tied back.
- *Can my child train barefoot?* — No, socks are required at all times on the trampoline.
