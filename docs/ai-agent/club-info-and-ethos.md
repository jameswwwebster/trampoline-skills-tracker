# Club Info & Ethos

## About Trampoline Life

Trampoline Life is a trampoline gymnastics club based in Newcastle, run by British Gymnastics qualified coaches. We welcome gymnasts from age 5 and focus on building skills in a fun, supportive environment — whether your child is just starting out or working towards competition.

We are affiliated with British Gymnastics and follow their welfare and safeguarding standards.

## Sessions

We run sessions on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Sessions are £6 per gymnast.

Booking is done through this app — browse available sessions on the calendar and complete the checkout.

**Example questions:**
- *When do you train?* — Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Check the calendar in the app for available sessions and times.
- *How do I book a session?* — Use the Bookings menu in the app to view the calendar and book sessions.
- *Is there a trial session?* — We don't offer trial sessions. Sessions are pay-as-you-go at £6 each, so you can come along and see how you get on.
- *Is there a joining fee?* — No joining fee.
