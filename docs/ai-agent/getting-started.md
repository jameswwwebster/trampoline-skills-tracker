# Getting Started

## How to Join

Trampoline Life is open to gymnasts from age 5. There is no joining fee and no trial session — sessions are pay-as-you-go at £6 each, so your child can come along and see how they get on from the start.

To get started, create an account at https://trampoline.life, add your child as a gymnast, and book a session through the app.

**Example questions:**
- *How do I sign my child up?* — Create an account at https://trampoline.life, add your child as a gymnast, and book a session through the app.
- *Is there a trial session?* — We don't offer trial sessions. Sessions are pay-as-you-go at £6 each, so you can just come along and see how you get on.
- *Is there a joining fee?* — No joining fee.
- *What age do you accept?* — We welcome gymnasts from age 5 upwards.
- *Do I need to commit to regular sessions?* — No. Sessions are pay-as-you-go. You book what you want, when you want.

## Booking Sessions

Once your account is set up, use the **Bookings** menu to view the calendar and book sessions. Select a date, choose the session and the gymnasts you want to book, and complete the checkout.

Sessions cost £6 per gymnast.

**Example questions:**
- *How do I book a session?* — Go to the Bookings menu in the app, pick a date on the calendar, select the session and your gymnasts, and check out.
- *How much does a session cost?* — £6 per gymnast per session.
- *When are sessions?* — We run sessions on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Check the calendar in the app for available times.

## British Gymnastics Membership

All gymnasts need a valid British Gymnastics (BG) membership number to continue booking after their first two sessions. You can get one at https://www.british-gymnastics.org

Once you have a number, add it to your child's profile on the **Account** page. It will be verified against British Gymnastics records, and you can continue booking during the 14-day verification period.

**Example questions:**
- *Does my child need a British Gymnastics membership?* — Yes, after their first two sessions. You can get one at https://www.british-gymnastics.org and add the number on the Account page.
- *When does my child need a BG number?* — Before their third session. The first two can be booked without one.

## What to Bring

- Comfortable sportswear
- Sports shoes
- Socks (required at all times on the trampoline)
- Long hair tied back
- A drink

**Example questions:**
- *What should my child wear?* — Comfortable sportswear and sports shoes. Socks are required on the trampoline.
- *Can my child train in bare feet?* — No, socks are required at all times on the trampoline.
