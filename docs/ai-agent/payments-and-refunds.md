# Payments & Refunds

## Paying for Sessions

Sessions are paid at checkout using a saved card or a new card. The price is £6 per session per gymnast.

**Example questions:**
- *How much does a session cost?* — Sessions are £6 per gymnast per session.
- *How do I pay?* — At checkout you can use a saved payment method or add a new card.

## Session Cancellations & Credits

You can cancel any confirmed booking from **My Bookings** in the Bookings menu.

- **Cancelling a future session (any day before today):** The amount paid is returned as a credit to your account, valid for one month. Credits are applied automatically at your next checkout.
- **Cancelling a session on the same day it takes place:** No credit is issued.

**Example questions:**
- *Can I get a refund if I cancel a session?* — Cancellations for future sessions return a credit to your account (valid one month). Same-day cancellations do not receive a credit.
- *Where does my credit go?* — It's added to your account automatically and applied at your next checkout.
- *How long does a credit last?* — One month from the date it was issued.
- *I cancelled a session but didn't get a credit — why?* — Same-day cancellations are not eligible for a credit.

## Memberships

Memberships are paid monthly in advance. You can cancel at any time and the membership will not renew. If you cancel mid-period, your membership remains active until the end of the period already paid for.

For pricing, contact us on WhatsApp at https://wa.me/447700149040 — the cost depends on how many sessions your gymnast attends each week.

**Example questions:**
- *Can I cancel my membership?* — Yes, at any time. It won't renew after cancellation and stays active until the end of the current paid period.
- *Do I get a refund if I cancel mid-month?* — No, but the membership stays active until the end of the period you've paid for.

## Competition Entry Fees

Competition entry fees are set by the coaches before the invitation is sent. Once paid, entries are submitted to the competition organiser and fees are generally non-refundable. Contact us on WhatsApp at https://wa.me/447700149040 if you need to discuss a withdrawal.

**Example questions:**
- *Can I get a refund for a competition entry?* — Competition fees are generally non-refundable once submitted. Message us on WhatsApp at https://wa.me/447700149040 and we'll check what's possible.

## Outstanding Charges

Any outstanding charges from the club appear under **My Charges** in the Bookings menu. Unpaid charges will block future bookings, so it's important to settle them promptly. Add charges to your cart and check out to pay.

**Example questions:**
- *I have an outstanding charge — will it affect my bookings?* — Yes, unpaid charges will eventually block new bookings. Go to My Charges in the Bookings menu and pay through the cart.
- *Where do I pay a charge?* — Under My Charges in the Bookings menu. Add it to your cart and check out.
